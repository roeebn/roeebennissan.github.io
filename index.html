Research

Synthetic biology and metabolic engineering

Building and testing microbial pathways that convert carbon and produce useful molecules with lower resource use. Focus on pathway design, genetic validation, and performance metrics.

Food systems and microbial protein

Quantifying how microbial protein can reduce land use and emissions in global diets. Work with systems models and collaborators to assess mitigation potential.

Biodiversity under climate change

Measuring how rising seas alter ranges of land vertebrates and prioritising protection. Emphasis on DEM alignment, exposure mapping, and scenario analysis.
